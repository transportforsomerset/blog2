The Taunton to Weston-super-Mare section of the railway is closed for the week.

The Taunton to Weston-super-Mare section of the railway is closed for the week

The Taunton to Weston-super-Mare section of the railway is closed for the week, starting from Monday 3rd until Friday 7th February 2025.

Network Rail have closed the section between Taunton, Bridgwater, Highbridge & Burnham and Weston-super-Mare, to allow them to repair railway bridges that cross the line in various places.

GWR services that usually operate between Taunton and Weston-super-Mare will be replaced by railway buses, whereas services that operate between the South West of England and London won't be affected.

CrossCrountry services will continue to operate, but will divert from Taunton via Westbury, with journey times being extended.
